Take home items.

Mismatch of material properties induce instabilities in soft matter.

Buckling patterns emerge as mechanical responses to growth in surface cells.

We can characterise the pattern features with very few macroscopic parameters.

Transgenics and Mutants provide a nice sandbox to inhbit or induce patterns.

Geometry of the system plays a fundamental role in pattern morphology.

Inducing patterns before the onset can provide insights into the nature of the ridges.
